About

Racing Innovations was founded in Knoxville TN in 1998. Between 1998 and 2010 we had custom parts and services shops in two locations, one located between Knoxville and Oak Ridge in a little community called "Solway" and another in a small warehouse district in West Knoxville TN off of Parkside Drive.

In those 10+ years we had the honor of partnering with several great government organizations, such as; The National Transportation Research Corporation (NTRC) and the TRC.

We also were invited to walk alongside the Diy Network when they produced the Television series "Tricked Out", the show ran for 4 seasons.

R.I. enjoyed many great partnerships, with companies like Suzuki of North America and several independent research and design companies. We were instrumental in several big changes to the automotive industry and we are listed on several patents.
